Book 1 · The Dova Sku Series

Transync

First mission to a new world. One soldier, his crew, and an alien civilization humanity never existed.

Humanity finally reaches a confirmed habitable exoplanet: Catora, sixty light-years away. Lieutenant Christopher Thompkins joins the expedition as an easy escape from a battered past, but when the Light Skipper arrives, the planet’s ruins and a long-silent discovery reveal a much older conflict. Will Chris and his crew be able to survive this new alien world and the surprise that is brings?

Book 2 · The Dova Sku Series

Ascendant

The ghosts of one planet follow him home—and the war for humanity spills into the wider galaxy.

In the aftermath of Catora, Chris and the surviving crew fight to get back to Earth aboard a captured warship, the Ascendant. Liam now lives fully in Chris’s mind, turning him into a walking interface between human instinct and ancient technology. Their path home cuts through fractured empires, wary allies, and worlds still scarred by Svixen campaigns. As politics, honor duels, and full-scale fleet battles collide, Chris must weld humans and alien warriors into a single unit—or watch the Svixen finish what they started long before Earth’s recorded history began.
