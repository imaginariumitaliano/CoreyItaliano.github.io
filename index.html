About the Author

Corey Italiano

Raised on a lakeshore in Idaho and forged by motorcycles, snowboards, and way too many sci-fi books, Corey writes high-energy stories about ordinary humans thrown into extraordinary alien wars.

Short version: Corey Italiano is a smart-home and security technician by day and a science fiction author by night. A lifelong sci-fi junkie and adrenaline addict, he’s broken more bones than he can count and carries titanium in both shoulders, but still chases speed on a snowboard or Harley whenever he can.

One afternoon, intending to write a song, he accidentally wrote the prologue to Transync. That “oops” turned into The Dova Sku Series—a brutal, hopeful saga about humans, sentient AIs, and the long shadow of an ancient war.

When he’s not writing, you’ll find him gaming, planning the next trip, or arguing about which alien civilization would actually survive first contact.

The Dova Sku Series
One soldier. One AI. A war older than humanity.

Humanity’s first interstellar mission reaches Catora, a lush exoplanet sixty light-years from Earth. The crew discovers they’ve stepped onto an old battlefield between uplifted worlds and a ruthless empire called the Svixen. Lieutenant Christopher Thompkins—navy SEAL, newly minted cyborg, and reluctant hero— ends up bonded to Liam, an ancient Transync station AI. Together, they become the Dova Sku: Hell Machine. Their job is simple and impossible—keep a handful of survivors alive, out-fight an empire that once nearly erased Earth, and find a way home before history repeats.
